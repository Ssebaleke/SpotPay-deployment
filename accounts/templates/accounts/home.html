Features
Everything you need to run your hotspot
From payment collection to analytics — SpotPay gives you the tools to grow your WiFi business.

Mobile Money Payments

Accept payments via MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money. Customers pay and get connected instantly.

Multi-Location Management

Manage multiple hotspot locations from a single dashboard. Each location gets its own portal and settings.

Voucher Generation

Generate and sell WiFi vouchers in bulk. Set custom durations and data limits for each package.

Revenue Analytics

Track daily, weekly and monthly revenue with beautiful charts. Know exactly how your business is performing.

SMS Notifications

Automatically notify customers when their session is activated. Keep them informed with SMS alerts.

MikroTik Integration

Seamlessly integrates with MikroTik routers. Auto-login customers after payment with zero manual work.
